Entry 10: Christmas Tree


Christmas Tree

This weekend helped me heal and refresh myself before heading into dead week and nearing finals because I put up Christmas decorations around the house and set up the Christmas tree. My favorite month of the year is December because of the upbeat Christmas songs, shining lights, and festive decoration. Beautiful light decorations have always made me feel excited and happy, so December is the perfect mood lifting month for me. This year was terribly difficult to get through, but decorating the house was a delight. Though building a 7.5 foot tree on your own isnâ€™t very easy or fun, I still enjoyed the process because I could keep my mind off the stresses of school. Going through the ornaments we have and remembering the memories behind each one was my favorite part of the process. When I finished everything and finally plugged in the cord for the tree, I was blown away by the multicolor display in front of me.
