Entry 9: Thanksgiving Break


Thanksgiving Break

This year’s Thanksgiving looked a lot different from usual. In the past, my mom, dad, and I would crowd around our kitchen table. We’d have a stuffed turkey, mashed potatoes, beans, pecan pie, and much more. We’d talk about stories from our pasts, the recent news, or whatever random topic came to our minds. I always appreciated the holiday for being able to have a close family lunch. This year, though, Thanksgiving passed a lot more quietly with no big dinner or stories. I did spend some time for myself, taking a break after several weeks of rigorous school. I watched the “Great Pretender” on Netflix and thoroughly enjoyed the mystery and suspense and humor from the show. I adored the show so much that I drew Dorothy from the show. Though I couldn’t have a Thanksgiving like I did in the past, I still had a reasonably fun break.
