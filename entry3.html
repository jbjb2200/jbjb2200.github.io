Entry 3: Piano Practice


Piano Practice

Recently for a couple of weeks, I’ve been working on practicing Debussy’s “Clair De Lune.” This is still a really rough rendition of the piece, but I’m practicing specifically the areas where my fingers slip a lot. Despite being a challenging piece for me, I enjoy playing it because I need to apply my problem solving skills to accomplish learning the piece well. I also love how magical Debussy’s pieces sound, so playing “Clair De Lune” helps me escape to another world for a short period of time.
