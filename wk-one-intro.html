Where I end up introducing who I am and meeting my classmates.

Week One Introduction

Hello! My name is Jessica Burroughs (she/her/hers), and I'm a freshman majoring in Computer Science. I'm hoping to also get a B.S. in Economics. I live in Yelm, Washington, which is a one hour and thirty minute drive to Seattle without bad traffic, so I have about a tourist-level familiarity with the large city. I'm always open to touring Seattle with other people after quarantine lifts. For this quarter, I'm staying home.

Currently, I love most topics related to CS, so I don't know what I want to do specifically as a job. My goal is to spend time in college exploring the different paths that I can do. I'm slightly leaning towards data science, for I'm involved in a small non-profit organization called Mind My Mind where I enjoy visualizing data that shows the impact of COVID-19 on mental health.

In my free time, I love drawing characters on my phone, reading psychology books, pretending to be a professional nature photographer, and watching movies. I have a particular soft spot for animated movies with entrancing music, such as "The Prince of Egypt" and "Anastasia." On a related note, music is everything to me. I constantly have music playing in my headphones, and I play piano and flute. I'm excited to express my interests through my assignments for this class and improve my persuasion skills by working with various modes along with writing.

My daily ritual to clear my mind is taking a one or two hour nap after I finish my classes or day's work. It helps me release the stress of receiving an abundance of information and prepares me to study and do homework.

Thank you for reading this introduction. I'm eager to get to know everyone in this class better!
