Entry 6: Haikyuu


Haikyuu

One of my favorite shows to watch is Haikyuu because it never fails to make me laugh out loud and feel motivated. Many people just brush the show off as a simple show about volleyball, but the messages that the author weaves into the story really resonate with me, especially as I’m starting to hear responses back from internships. The author shows the journey of the main team working hard and training after their losses and aiming for the top, but the team never wins the big tournament. Unexpectedly, the author never even shows the faces of the winners. I interpret this as the author telling readers that life is never about solely winning, but about picking oneself up from a loss and looking up. This is what’s pushing me to keep applying to internships, working on improving my resume and essays to hopefully one day receive a positive response.
