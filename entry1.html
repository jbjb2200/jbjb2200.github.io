Entry 1: Favorite Weather

Favorite Weather

As winter draws closer, the weather is getting colder and more rain falls from the sky. I’ve become accustomed to the rain due to living in Washington all of my life, but I still prefer the sun much more. My ultimate favorite weather, though, is when the sun shines through the clouds after a rainstorm. I love jumping through the puddles while enjoying the view of a rainbow and breathing in the fresh nature smell. I’m also reminded that there are brighter moments after chaos. Last week was one of the most stressful and upsetting weeks of my life due to a tragedy in the family right before university started, but I realize that my situation will improve, and my sun will poke through the dark clouds again.
